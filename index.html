QScience Connect XML files

This is exactly what it says on the tin. A dump of all of our articles published in QScience Connect and available for you to do whatever you wish to - as long as the original source is cited (in this case, it will be the original publication on QScience Connect.

Please let us know if you find these useful. We are wondering about how we can use this for authoring and reviewing and versioning academic research articles, so if you're interested in that, get in touch
